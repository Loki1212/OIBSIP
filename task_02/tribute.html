Jamsetji Tata Tribute

About Jamsetji Tata

Jamsetji Tata was an Indian industrialist who founded the Tata Group, one of India's largest conglomerates. Born in Navsari, Gujarat in 1839, he began his career as a trader before moving on to the textile industry. His first venture was a textile mill in Nagpur, which he started with his son Dorabji Tata.

Over the years, Jamsetji Tata diversified his business interests, and the Tata Group grew to include companies in industries such as steel, power, chemicals, and hospitality. He was also a philanthropist, and founded several institutions, including the Indian Institute of Science in Bangalore and the Tata Memorial Hospital in Mumbai.
